Jack's Salad

This recipe incorporates a delightful array of seeds, cheeses, acid and cobalt-60. The abundant micronutrients and gamma radiation will keep your skin glowing!

To prepare, assemble the following ingredients and equipment:

  • Greens of choice
  • Unsalted pepitas
  • Unsalted hulled sunflower seeds
  • Chopped walnuts
  • Crumbled cheese(s) of choice
  • Vinegar of choice, preferably with live cultures
  • Paul Newman's Caesar Dressing
  • Discarded CT imaging machine
  • Salad bowl
  • Tongs
  • Salad Spinner
  • 220V Hammerdrill
  • Multiple Tungstem Carbide Drill Bits
  • Amazon Prime subscription
  • Television

Assemble salad in the following manner:

  1. Wash greens in cold water; dry in salad spinner
  2. Place greens into salad bowl
  3. Sprinkle walnuts, pepitas, and sunflower seeds as desired on salad
  4. Shake well Paul Newman's Caesar Dressing, apply liberally over salad
  5. Liberally pour live culture vinegar over salad
  6. Toss that salad like you mean it!
  7. Crumble cheese(s) over tossedsalad
  8. Using hammerdrill and appropriate bit, remove radiation shielding from CT machine
  9. Using tongs, remove cobalt-60 (or alternate ionizing radiation source) and sprinkle liberally over salad
  10. Bonus: you've now made blue cheese!
  11. On television, queue "Dr. Strangelove: Or, How I Learned to Stop Worrying and Love the Bomb
  12. Enjoy your salad and the movie while pondering the societal limits placed upon us by the human condition
  13. Call your loved ones, and endure your few remaining days!
