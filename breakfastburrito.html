Breakfast Burrito

This instructional guide will help you to craft a brilliantly colorful yet gastronomically vacant breakfast burrito.

If you are unsatisfied with the result, feel free to add food dye to your eyeballs :)

You will require the following ingredients:

  • Green dye
  • Red Dye
  • Yellow Dye
  • 200g Textured Fungal Protein Mash
  • 20g Micronutrient Paste
  • 1 unit Recycled Carbon Tortilla
  • Hot Sauce of choice

You will require the following equipment:

  • Skillet and Heat Source
  • One Large Mixing Bowl
  • Three Small Mixing Bowls
  • Spatula

Once all ingredients have been obtained, follow these steps:

  1. Preheat Skillet on medium-high heat
  2. Combine Textured Fungal Protein and Micronutrient Paste
  3. Separate equally into three bowls
  4. Add 10 drops of food dye to bowls, one color per bowl
  5. Thorougly mix food dye into protient/paste mixture
  6. Combine all mixtures in skillet, allowing to mix but remain heterogenous
  7. Cook for 3 minutes, stirring occasionally
  8. Remove from heat and allow to rest for 1 minute
  9. Place cooked protein into Carbon Tortilla
  10. Apply hot sauce as desired
  11. Roll burrito in whatever way seems best to you
  12. Throw away burrito, and eat my dust! Deuces, cabooses!
